FoodTech Design Directions

Explore 7 purpose-built views, each designed for its persona's environment, device, and mental model.

Design Philosophy: "One tap, many ripples." Each view is purpose-built for its persona — not a filtered version of a generic dashboard. Kitchen views use dark theme with large touch targets. Office views use light theme with dense layouts. Mobile views are minimal and glanceable.

Kitchen Views (Dark Theme)

Dark backgrounds reduce glare under bright kitchen lighting. Large touch targets (48dp+) work with wet/gloved hands. High contrast status colors are visible from across the kitchen. Attention-driven UI: problems glow, healthy operations fade.

Office & Mobile Views (Light Theme)

Clean white backgrounds for focused desktop work. Dense data tables for supplier batch operations. Mobile-optimized customer tracker — one screen, instant comprehension. Standard web conventions for management and admin views.

Attention-Driven UI System

The core UX innovation: problems glow, healthy operations fade. Information finds you.

How it works: Every entity on screen (station, ticket, order) has an attention state. Healthy items reduce opacity to 70% and have no animation. Warning items return to full opacity with a slow pulse (2s cycle) and subtle glow. Critical items add a faster pulse (1s) and brighter glow. The result: your eye is naturally drawn to what needs attention without active scanning.
